Mihai’s Grand Road Trip

What, you may ask, does this “Road Trip” truly mean ? Is it simply a journey from point A to point B ? Hardly.

This adventure was born of a deep and abiding passion of mine

travelling, exploring, and expanding my knowledge beyond what I already know.But most of all, it stems from my greatest love.

Cars and the pure joy of driving.

This is why I’ve dreamed up this road trip in the first place, to drive through countless countries in my favourite car, with my closest friends,

creating unforgettable memories and experiences that can’t be replicated.

The plan ? To chart a course across the vast, beautiful canvas of continental Europe.

A land brimming with breathtaking architecture, rich history, vibrant culture, mouth watering cuisines,and countless surprises awaiting discovery.

Yet, it’s more than just a road trip about my passions for cars, travel, and learning.

It’s about something deeper. Cherished moments with my favourite people.

It’s about being together in places we’ve never seen before,

laughing until our sides hurt,

raising a glass (or a bottle) in celebration,

and concocting brilliant, sometimes reckless, plans as we wind along Europe’s scenic roads.

Call it childish if you like, but truly, what better way is there to live ?

This isn’t just a road trip. It’s a chance to seize the best moments of our lives, with the people who matter most,

while the road stretches endlessly ahead of us.



Now, if you’ve been invited to join this odyssey, congratulations, you’ve made it into Mihai’s inner circle. You are, without question, one of my most cherished individuals, someone I hold in high regard.

As you scroll further, you’ll uncover all the details you’ll need, the map of destinations, the crew who’ll share the ride, who will drive and who will be the passenger princess.

And the little luxuries we’ve planned to make this journey as unforgettable as the destinations themselves.



So, my friends, prepare yourselves. For this isn’t just a road trip. It’s an adventure. One that we’ll reminisce about for years to come.

Buckle up. The road to memories begins here.
