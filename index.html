Support & Filming Crew

A team of 16 will join us, fulfilling these roles:

  • Nine Drivers: 6 for the crew cars, plus 3 additional support drivers. They’ll ensure smooth driving shifts and handle any vehicle related needs during the journey.
  • Two Camera Operators: Using Sony cameras to capture every moment. They’ll document the trip’s highlights, from scenic drives to group celebrations.
  • Two Drone Operators: Flying DJI drones for cinematic aerial footage. They’ll capture stunning overhead views of the convoy and landscapes, especially during campsites.
  • Two Assistants: Managing bookings, safety, and logistics. They’ll handle reservations, ensure safety protocols, and manage funds for gas, tolls, and stops.
  • One Photographer: With a Sony Alpha 1 to preserve memories. Hey’ll take high quality stills of the group, cars, and destinations for lasting keepsakes.

The crew will use top-tier gear to ensure a seamless, stress-free journey.

Trip Guidelines

These are the essential guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip:

  • Clean Interiors: Keep the vehicle's interiors clean. Maintain the interior of the cars clean at all times.
  • No Drugs: No drug use at any time. Ensures a safe and focused journey for all participants.
  • No Alcohol: No alcohol consumption while driving. If you are a driver, no alcohol unless you won't drive for the next 10 hours.
  • Respect Boundaries: no sexual activities. Fosters a respectful and comfortable group dynamic.
  • Seatbelt Safety: wear seatbelts at all times. Ensures everyone’s safety during the trip, you never know what can happen.
  • Punctuality: adhere to departure times. Keeps the group on schedule for a smooth journey.
  • Moderate Music Volume: Keep music volume at a reasonable level for everyone's comfort. Ensures a pleasant experience for all passengers.
  • Trash Disposal: Dispose of the trash responsibly. Keeps the environment clean and respects shared spaces.
  • Rotate Seating: Rotate seating regularly to foster team bonding. Encourages interaction and camaraderie among group members.
  • No Food in Cars: Eat only outside of the car, no food consumption inside, except for drinking. Prevents messes and maintains vehicle cleanliness.
  • No Vehicle Alterations: Avoid adding stickers or altering with the car's interior or exterior. Preserves the condition of the vehicles throughout the trip.

Following these rules ensures a seamless and respectful journey for all.

Equipment

Each participant and vehicle will be equipped with the following for a seamless journey:

  • Personal Devices: Every participant receives a Laptop, Tablet, Smartphone, and Headphones. These devices, with accompanying accessories, ensure entertainment and connectivity throughout the trip.
  • Walkie-Talkies: Everyone will receive a radio for seamless communication between cars. Enables real-time coordination and updates across the convoy.
  • Itinerary Guides: Each vehicle will be equipped with a specific itinerary. Keeps everyone on track with planned routes and stops.
  • Emergency Kits: Each vehicle will be equipped with an emergency kit for safety. Provides essential supplies for handling unexpected situations.
  • Pillows: Each vehicle will be equipped with three or more pillows. Ensures passenger comfort during long drives and rest stops.

This equipment ensures comfort, safety, and connectivity for all participants.
